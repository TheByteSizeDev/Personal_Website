A Little About Me

Hey there! I'm Sydney Noh and tackling creative endeavors in dynamic environments has been the backbone of my career. Having spent four years in television production roles for shows like HGTV’s Flip or Flop Nashville and America’s Got Talent, my passion lies in developing a cohesive and powerful narrative to create a genuine connection with the user through media. I am now pivoting towards building these human stories with code, and I am loving every minute of it! Take a look at how my production moxy translates into coding chops.
